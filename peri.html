Computer Peripherals

Peripherals are the components of a computer which communicate with the processor (CPU) but are not located directly on the motherboard.

 Some peripherals are linked by expansion cards in the expansion slots on the motherboard connected directly on the ports via cables attached on them.

Examples of such periherals are: keyboard,mouse,printer,scanner etc.


NB: Peripherals are simply, the components that are not in-built in the motherboard. A component that is built in the motherboard already by the manufacturer, is not a peripheral! eg:processor,cmos battery etc.

Keyboard-This has keypad and keys to handle specific functions required by the computer. This resembles a typewriter.

Categories of Keyboard

1.Alphanumeric Keys (alphabets & numbers) comprises of alphabets (A-Z),numbers (0-9) and other characters.

2.Special keys: they perform specific functions Eg: Enter(return),Backspace,Delete,PageUp(pgup),capslock,shift,Alt(alternate,etc.

3. Function keys; F1-F12,F1-F10
